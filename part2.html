Chapter 4: The Superpower Duality: United States vs. China

While the U.S. maintains a significant lead in private AI investment, China is leveraging massive state-backed funds to rapidly close the technological capability gap, creating a tense duality at the top of the global AI landscape.

Investment and Infrastructure

In 2024, private AI investment in the U.S. ($109.1 billion) was nearly 12 times greater than in China ($9.3 billion). By Q1 2025, total corporate AI investment in the U.S. reached $189 billion, representing 65% of the global total. Major infrastructure projects include the $500B Microsoft/OpenAI "Stargate Project" and China's $47.5B semiconductor fund.
