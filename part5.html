Chapter 13: The Hardware Backbone

The AI arms race is built on a foundation of unprecedented hardware investment and innovation. Hyperscalers are projected to spend over $220 billion on AI infrastructure by 2025. However, this explosion in computational power brings a critical challenge: the immense and growing demand for energy and water.

The Semiconductor & Edge AI Arms Race

  • ASICs' market share to rise from 22% to 30% by 2025, cutting into GPU dominance.
  • Edge AI market projected to reach $200 billion with a 24% CAGR through 2030.
  • Over 970 million smartphones expected to ship with on-board NPUs in 2025.

The Energy Imperative

  • Data center power demand could surge to over 1,000 TWh by 2030, driven by AI.
  • Next-gen GPUs expected to reach 1,200 watts, up from 400 watts in 2022.
  • Training a model like GPT-3 can consume 700,000 liters of fresh water.
